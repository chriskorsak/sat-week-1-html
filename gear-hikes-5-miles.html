Gear for Hikes under 5 miles

You've seen him on the trail. It's hiker guy. He looks like a walking REI catalog. He's checking his GPS waypoints every two feet while taking a sip from his camelback and putting on his 4.56oz puffy coat at the hint of a breeze. It's soooo light! It also cost $300 dollars and will rip if you graze a blackberry bush, bro!

Oh ya, I was supposed to provide gear tips. Here are what I think is a great list of gear you should consider bringing on a hike that's less than 5 miles:

  • Water
  • Sunscreen
  • Map (preferably of the location you are hiking in)
  • Snacks More food tips here!
  • Space Blanket
  • Sunscreen
  • A hiking partner (never hike alone, bro)
